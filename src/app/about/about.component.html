About TypeRace
What
TypeRace is an attractive and easy to use website, on which you can test yourself and find out your writing speed. After entering your name, you will be forwarded to the main page, where you’ll be typing the displayed words for one minute. When you have finished, you’ll be able to see your Words Per Minute (WPM) calculated with this formula.
Why
I created this website as preparation to improve my knowledge on the technologies, ex. Angular, that I’ll be needing during my internship starting in august 2020.
Who
I’m Noah Salvi, an 18-year-old Software Developer in training, based in Berne, Switzerland. At the time when I came up with the idea for TypeRace, my friends and I, were learning the touch-typing system, with the help of the internet. That’s how I came in contact with websites that taught you touch typing or even sites, that tested your writing speed. After using them on a daily basis, I decided to make an improved version of the sites I was visiting and to also improve my website skills at the same time.
